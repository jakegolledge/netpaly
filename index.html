WHY CYBER SECURITY


In today's digitalizing economy, cyber crime is now larger than all other forms of organised crime collectively.

It is the biggest threat to an evolving business, encompassing both pragmatic risks to competitive advantage, or integrity, and the associated financial risks of compliance failure, including fines from bodies such as the ICO.

SMEs are particularly vulnerable. Taking measures to adaquetely protect data and comply with regulation such as GDPR can be difficult, however the significance of these measures only grow with time.

Implementing a system to reduce cyber risk doesn't have to be costly or complex.
