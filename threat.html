NET PALADIN

THREAT MODELLING


Net Paladin will investigate the organisational capacity and build threat models to identify key cyber risk, mitigations and potential actions.

- Assess adaquancy of security design
- Apply tools, techniques and systems to identify vulnerabilities in the enterprise
- Improve enterprise ability to increase integrity and reduce moral hazard
- Document and advice of best practise in reponse to findings
- In line with OWASP and GDPR guidelines
